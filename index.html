the helpless doorknob: a shuffled story

by Edward Gorey

My dad and stepmom gave me The Helpless Doorknob, a deck of 20 cards that had one sentence with illustration from Edward Gorey. According to the box, there are 2,432,902,069,736,640,000 different versions of the story. This app is my thank-you note.

Click Helpless Doorknob tombstone for one version. Every time you click, a new shuffled story will appear so that you can compare them.
